FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before working with us.

How long does it take to build my website? +
Most websites are designed, developed, and delivered within 5–7 business days. Complex projects with e-commerce or custom features may take 10–14 days. We always agree on a timeline upfront before starting work.
Do I need to pay anything upfront? +
Yes, we require a 50% advance payment to begin work, with the remaining 50% due before the final handover. This protects both parties and ensures project commitment. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and Razorpay.
Will my website work on mobile phones? +
Absolutely. Every website we build is mobile-first — meaning we design for mobile screens first and scale up. Over 80% of your customers will visit from their phone, so this is non-negotiable for us.
What if I want changes after the website is live? +
During the project, revisions are unlimited. After launch, we offer monthly maintenance plans starting at ₹999/month that include content updates, small design tweaks, and technical support. Bigger changes are quoted separately.
Do you provide hosting and domain? +
Yes! All our packages include free domain registration and hosting for 1 year (₹1,500+ value). We deploy on Vercel for blazing-fast global performance. After year 1, renewal is approximately ₹1,200–1,500/year.
Can you manage my Meta Ads after the website is live? +
Yes! Meta Ads management is available as an add-on or included in our Growth and Premium packages. We handle everything — audience research, creative design, copywriting, campaign setup, and monthly performance reporting. Ad spend is separate from our management fee.
I'm a small business. Is this worth it for me? +
100% yes. A ₹4,000 website that brings in 5 new customers a month is paying for itself every few weeks. We work with solo business owners, small shops, and local services who want to grow — your size doesn't matter, your ambition does.
