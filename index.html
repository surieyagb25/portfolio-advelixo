FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build my website?

Landing pages are delivered in 5 days. Business websites take 7–10 days. Redesigns usually 7 days. We stick to deadlines — your time matters.

Do I need to provide content and photos?

Ideally yes — your own photos build more trust. But if you don't have them, we'll use high-quality stock images and write your copy based on our discovery call. No extra charge.

Will my website work on mobile?

100%. Every website we build is mobile-first. We test on iPhone, Android, tablet, and desktop before delivery. It'll look perfect everywhere.

What happens after the website is delivered?

You get 30 days of free support after delivery. We're available via WhatsApp for any bugs, small changes, or questions. After that, we offer affordable maintenance plans.

Can you also run Meta ads for my business?

Yes! We manage Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns end-to-end — from audience research and ad creatives to daily optimisation and monthly reporting. Ask about our bundle discounts when combining ads with website services.

How do I pay?

We accept UPI, bank transfer, and Razorpay. Payment is split: 50% upfront to begin work, 50% on final delivery before we hand over the files.
